# AISINDO Symposium 2023 — Website

Static website for the 2023 AISINDO Symposium on Accounting Information
Systems and Digital Transformation, held 15–16 November 2023 at the
Department of Information Systems, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember
(ITS), Surabaya, Indonesia.

## Pages
- index.html — overview, keynote speakers, venue
- cfp.html — call for papers
- committee.html — programme committee
- schedule.html — two-day programme
- proceedings.html — accepted papers and review process

## Assets
- style.css — warm tropical Indonesian palette (teal / coral / sand / kayu jati)
- images/ — speaker portraits, venue, conference scene, networking, registration

Held under the auspices of AISINDO — Yayasan Asosiasi Sistem Informasi
Indonesia, the Official Indonesia Chapter of the Association for
Information Systems (AIS). Proceedings published in CEUR-WS · ISSN 1613-0073.
