Call for Papers

The Programme Committee of the 2023 AISINDO Symposium invites original research contributions on accounting information systems and the digital transformation of organisations operating in Indonesia, the ASEAN region, and other emerging economies. Accepted papers appear in the open-access CEUR Workshop Proceedings (ISSN 1613-0073).

Tracks and Topics

The symposium welcomes empirical, conceptual, design-science, and case-study research. Submissions are organised in four thematic tracks:

Track 1 — Accounting Information Systems & ERP

  • AIS adoption, post-implementation, and benefits realisation
  • ERP customisation in emerging-economy SMEs and family firms
  • Cloud-based accounting platforms and SaaS adoption
  • Process mining for financial workflows

Track 2 — Audit, Assurance & Internal Controls

  • Continuous auditing and audit analytics
  • Robotic process automation (RPA) in audit engagements
  • Internal controls in digital and hybrid AIS environments
  • Cybersecurity assurance for financial information systems

Track 3 — Digital Transformation & Strategy

  • Digital transformation roadmaps for emerging-economy enterprises
  • Capability building and human-capital implications
  • Platform ecosystems and inter-organisational information flows
  • Fintech, payments, and digital-finance adoption

Track 4 — Emerging Technologies & Reporting

  • Blockchain and distributed ledgers for accounting
  • AI/ML for forecasting, anomaly detection, and decision support
  • XBRL, integrated reporting, and ESG disclosure systems
  • Sustainability accounting and triple-bottom-line information systems

Submission Guidelines

Authors submit full papers of 4 to 10 pages formatted in the single-column CEUR-ART style. Submissions are managed through EasyChair and undergo double-blind peer review by at least two members of the Programme Committee. Papers must report original work not under simultaneous review elsewhere.

Format

CEUR-ART single-column, 10pt

Length

4–10 pages including references

Review

Double-blind, ≥ 2 reviewers

Language

English

Publication

Accepted and presented papers will be published as an open-access volume of CEUR Workshop Proceedings (CEUR-WS.org), indexed by DBLP, Scopus, and Google Scholar, under ISSN 1613-0073. Each paper is assigned a stable URL and listed in the volume's table of contents alongside front matter prepared by the Programme Committee Chair.

Best Paper Award. The Programme Committee will recognise one outstanding paper with the AISINDO 2023 Best Paper Award, evaluated on theoretical contribution, methodological rigour, and regional relevance.
