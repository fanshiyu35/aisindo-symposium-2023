Call for Papers

The 2023 AISINDO Symposium invites submissions of original research papers addressing the intersection of Accounting Information Systems and digital transformation, with particular interest in the challenges and opportunities facing organisations in emerging economies.

📌 Submission Deadline

30 September 2023

Notifications: 20 October 2023  |  Camera-ready: 5 November 2023

Submission Tracks

Track 1: Digital Transformation of AIS

Cloud-based accounting, ERP modernisation, digital platforms for financial reporting, and organisational change in accounting functions.

Track 2: Emerging Technologies in Accounting

Blockchain, AI/ML, robotic process automation, and process mining in audit, assurance, and financial management contexts.

Track 3: Data Analytics and Business Intelligence

Big data in accounting, predictive analytics for financial decision-making, continuous auditing, and visual analytics.

Track 4: Governance, Risk, and Compliance

IT governance frameworks, cybersecurity in financial systems, regulatory technology, and internal control in digital environments.

Submission Guidelines

  • Papers must be 4–6 pages in length, following the CEUR-WS single-column format.
  • All submissions undergo double-blind peer review by at least two members of the Program Committee.
  • Accepted papers will be published in the symposium proceedings via CEUR Workshop Proceedings (ISSN 1613-0073) and made available as open-access publications.
  • At least one author of each accepted paper must register and present at the symposium.
