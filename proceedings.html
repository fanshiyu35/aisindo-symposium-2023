Proceedings of the 2023 AISINDO Symposium

The peer-reviewed proceedings of the 2023 AISINDO Symposium on Accounting Information Systems and Digital Transformation are published as an open-access volume in CEUR Workshop Proceedings (CEUR-WS.org), ISSN 1613-0073, December 2023. Each contribution has undergone double-blind review by at least two members of the Programme Committee.

Review Process

Each submission to the 2023 AISINDO Symposium underwent rigorous double-blind peer review by at least two members of the Programme Committee, with an extended panel of 24 sub-reviewers supporting evaluation. Conflicts were adjudicated by the Programme Committee Chair. On-site registration and badge collection took place in the lobby of the IS Building on the morning of 15 November.
