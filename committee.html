Program Committee

The Programme Committee for the 2023 AISINDO Symposium brings together accounting information systems and digital transformation scholars from across Indonesia, Malaysia, Thailand, Vietnam, and the broader Southeast Asian research community. The committee oversees the double-blind peer-review process and curates the symposium programme.

Reviewing Panel

In addition to the Programme Committee, an extended reviewing panel of 24 sub-reviewers supported the double-blind evaluation of submissions. Each paper received at least two independent reviews, with conflicts adjudicated by the Programme Committee Chair.

Acknowledgement. The Programme Committee gratefully acknowledges the Department of Information Systems at ITS Surabaya for hosting the symposium, and the AISINDO Executive Council for institutional support.
