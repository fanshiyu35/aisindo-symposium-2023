Symposium Schedule

All sessions take place at the Department of Information Systems building, ITS Sukolilo Campus, Surabaya. Times are in Western Indonesian Time (WIB, UTC+7). The programme includes two keynote addresses, six paper sessions, a panel discussion, and a poster reception.
